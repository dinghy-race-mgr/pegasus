STARCROSS YACHT CLUB
2019 Sailing Programme
This is the programme of events and duty allocations for 2019. Please check and make a note of duty allocations carefully. You will be notified of Galley and Bar duties separately. You will also get a notification and reminder through the Dutyman system. Please use Dutyman in the first instance for organising swaps if you are unable to fulfil your allocated duty.
Club Duties
It is a requirement of membership that all club members are expected to take undertake at least two club duties per year . Please make a point of checking when your duties are programmed. If you are unable to undertake the allocated duty, it is your responsibility to arrange cover or a swap with another member so that this duty is properly covered. Those on duty should arrive at the club at least one hour before the scheduled event.
